Mission St. Louis

Lift for Life Academy

Failure is not an option. Our singular priority is to have our students succeed during their journey at LFLA and after they graduate. We provide the foundation for their success and they pursue the journey with our encouragement and support.

Read More

PotBangerz

PotBangerz is a St. Louis based, 501c3 family of community leaders who fight injustice by uplifting the community, meeting nutritional needs, helping our unhoused families navigate their way to permanent housing, and advocating for them when they need it the most. When we have rebuilt and transformed the lives of our unhoused families we can say mission accomplished.

Read More

Ali Academy: Greatness. Within. Reach

Ali Academy empowers scholars to achieve academic, professional, communal and personal greatness by focusing on relevance, rigor, and righteousness.

Read More

North Side Community School

The mission of North Side Community School — a #1 preschool through 8th-grade charter school serving the most violent and impoverished neighborhoods in St. Louis — is to improve each student’s opportunities in education and in life by developing the skills, knowledge, and personal qualities necessary for success.

Read More

St. Patrick Center

St. Patrick Center provides opportunities for self-sufficiency and dignity to people who are homeless or at risk of becoming homeless. Individuals and families build permanent, positive change in their lives through safe and affordable housing, sound mental and physical health, and employment and financial stability.

Read More

Pianos for People

Pianos for People provides free access to the transformative power of music to those who need it most – children and families with limited resources, and the organizations that serve them.
