  • Mission: St. Louis

    Affordable Christmas Toy Drive!

    Donate new, unwrapped gifts and toys by November 30th to help the 14th annual Affordable Christmas program.

    Last year, Mission St. Louis was able to supply gifts for over 1,000 children in the St. Louis Region, and this year they aim to serve even more.

    There are WalMart, Target, and Amazon gift registries for suggested gifts. You can also make monetary donations at the Affordable Christmas donation page.

  • St. Patrick Center

    Cold Weather Drive

    Cold weather will be here soon, please help those in our community by donating new and gently used coats, scarves, hats, and gloves.

    Donations can be placed in donation boxes in the CIC buildings in the Cortex district at CIC@CET 20 South Sarah, at 4240 Duncan, and at 4220 Duncan buildings. You can also make monetary donations at the St. Patrick Center donation page. We will be collecting now through Friday, December 4th.
